Perfect Omelet

Start your day with our fluffy and delicious omelet recipe. This classic breakfast dish is easy to make and customizable to your taste preferences.

Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 5 mins
Serves: 1

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional fillings: cheese, ham, vegetables, etc.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter.
  3. Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the egg mixture.
  4. As the eggs set, use a spatula to gently push the cooked edges toward the center of the pan, tilting the pan to allow uncooked egg to flow to the edges.
  5. When the eggs are almost set but still slightly wet on top, add your chosen fillings to one half of the omelet.
  6. Fold the other half of the omelet over the fillings.
  7. Cook for another minute, then slide onto a plate and serve immediately.

Tips for the Perfect Omelet

  • Use fresh eggs for the best flavor and texture.
  • Don't overbeat the eggs - a few air bubbles are good for a fluffy omelet.
  • Use a non-stick pan for easy flipping and cleaning.
  • Experiment with different fillings to find your favorite combination.
