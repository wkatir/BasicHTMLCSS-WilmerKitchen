Creamy Pasta

A comforting pasta dish that's perfect for any occasion.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 400g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Cook for 3-4 minutes until slightly thickened.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat with the sauce.
  5. Gradually add the Parmesan cheese, stirring until melted and well combined.
  6. If the sauce is too thick, add some of the reserved pasta water to reach desired consistency.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley and additional Parmesan if desired.

Tips for the Perfect Creamy Pasta

  • Use high-quality Parmesan cheese for the best flavor.
  • Don't overcook the pasta - it should be al dente.
  • Reserve some pasta water to adjust the sauce consistency.
  • Experiment with adding vegetables or protein for a more substantial meal.
