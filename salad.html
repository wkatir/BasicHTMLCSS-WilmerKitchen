Fresh Garden Salad

A crisp and refreshing salad packed with nutrients.

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 0 mins
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cucumber, tomatoes, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the top.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh garden salad!

Tips for the Perfect Garden Salad

  • Use a variety of greens for different textures and flavors.
  • Wash and dry your greens thoroughly to ensure a crisp salad.
  • Add nuts or seeds for extra crunch and nutrition.
  • Experiment with different dressings to find your favorite combination.
