Juicy Grilled Steak

Learn how to grill the perfect steak every time.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 10-15 mins
Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks (1-inch thick)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Remove steaks from refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking to bring to room temperature.
  2. Preheat grill to high heat.
  3. Rub steaks with olive oil and season generously with salt, pepper, minced garlic, and rosemary.
  4. Place steaks on the hot grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare.
  5. Remove steaks from grill and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  6. Slice against the grain and serve immediately.

Tips for the Perfect Grilled Steak

  • Choose high-quality, well-marbled steaks for the best flavor.
  • Always let your steak come to room temperature before grilling.
  • Use a meat thermometer to ensure desired doneness.
  • Let the steak rest after grilling to retain its juices.
